GM12891_cmp_GM12892.H3K4me3_profile_bins.xls is generated by applying MAnorm2_utils 1.0.0 to H3K4me3 ChIP-seq samples for GM12891 and GM12892 LCLs (lymphoblastoid cell lines). Each LCL is associated with three biological replicates.

GM12891_cmp_GM12892.H3K4me3_hieMA.xls records normalized log2 read counts for the samples, which are derived by the hierarchical MA normalization method.

Rows of both files correspond to each other one by one.
